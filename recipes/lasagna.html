LASAGNA DISH

Descriptions

This comfort food favorite layers robust tomato sauce, ground beef and a mozzarella-ricotta cheese mixture between sheets of pasta. This baked dish is perfect for everything from busy weeknight dinners to parties and entertaining.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 package Anna Traditional Lasagne (1 lb)
  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 1 jar Cento All Purpose Pasta Sauce (26 oz)
  • 1 cup Water
  • 1 Egg, beaten
  • 1 lb Ricotta Cheese
  • 8 oz Mozzarella Cheese, grated

STEPS

  1. Making the Meat Sauce
  2. Cook (or Don't Cook) Your Noodles
  3. Prep the Cheese and Finish the Sauce
  4. Assembling!
